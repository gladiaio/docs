## What is Zapier?

Zapier is a no-code automation platform that connects thousands of apps together. With the Gladia integration on Zapier, you can build automated workflows (called Zaps) that trigger transcription jobs, process results, and route them to other apps â€” all without writing any code.

## What you can build

- Automated transcription of audio files uploaded to cloud storage
- Workflows that send transcription results to Slack, email, or Notion
- Meeting recording pipelines that trigger on calendar events
- Content repurposing workflows from audio to text
