---
title: "Overview"
description: "Features overview of Gladia's Asynchronous speech-to-text (STT) API."
---

| **Setting**                                                 | **Description**                                                                  |
|-------------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| [Language(s)](/chapters/settings/language-options)          | Configure the model language and/or enable multi-languages transcription.        |
| [Custom Vocabulary](/chapters/settings/vocabulary-spelling) | Enhance the transcription precision of words you know.                           |
| [Diarization](/chapters/settings/diarization)               | Identify speakers during the conversation.                                       |
| [Formating](/chapters/settings/formatting)                  | Improve the transcript readability.                                              |
| [Media Input](/chapters/settings/media)                     | Define audio media's parameters sent to the API.                                 |
| [Subtitles](/chapters/pre-recorded-stt/captions)            | Automatically generate WebVTT & SRT captions content.                            |
| [Word-level Timestamps](/chapters/settings/word-timestamps) | Know the exact timestamp for each word, giving you a more precise transcription. |
| [Custom Metadata](/chapters/settings/custom-metadata)       | Add metadata to track and filter your requests.                                  |

<br/>

| **Audio Intelligence**                                                                  | **Description**                                                                                            |
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| [Sentiment and Emotion Analysis](/chapters/audio-intelligence/pages/sentiment-analysis) | Extract sentiments and emotions from the audio, like confusion or interest.                                |
| [Name Entity Recognition](/chapters/audio-intelligence/pages/named-entity-recognition)  | Automatically identifies and categorizes key information in the audio, like phone number or email address. |
| [Summarization](/chapters/audio-intelligence/pages/summarization)                       | Get important information from your conversation.                                                          |
| [Translation](/chapters/audio-intelligence/pages/translation)                           | Translate your transcribed content.                                                                        |
| [Audio to LLM](/chapters/audio-intelligence/pages/audio-to-llm)                         | Ask any follow-up question or analysis on the conversation.                                                |
| [Chapterization](/chapters/audio-intelligence/pages/chapterization)                     | Segment your audio into distinct chapters.                                                                 |
| [Moderation](/chapters/audio-intelligence/pages/moderation)                             | Detect potentially inappropriate content in your audio.                                                    |
| [Data Extraction](/chapters/audio-intelligence/pages/structured-data-extraction)        | Get nested json structure directly from the audio conversation.                                            |
